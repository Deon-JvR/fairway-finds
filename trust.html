Know how

How the Fairway Finds process works.

The platform is built so buyers, sellers, and Fairway Finds all have a clear record of the item, seller details, paid visibility, and approval status.

  1. 01

    Create and verify your profile

    Users sign up, confirm their email, add contact and address details, then wait for admin approval before buying or selling.

  2. 02

    List golf gear for review

    Sellers upload photos, item details, condition, price, and location. Fairway Finds reviews listings before they go live.

  3. 03

    Buyer contacts the seller directly

    Fairway Finds connects both parties. Payments and deliveries are arranged directly between buyer and seller.

  4. 04

    Verify before paying

    Buyers should verify the seller and item before paying. Fairway Finds does not handle payment or delivery for direct sales.

Safety tips

Never pay before verifying the seller and the item.

Fairway Finds connects buyers and sellers, but payments and deliveries are arranged directly between the parties.

Ask for proof

Request extra photos, serial numbers where available, and a short video if the item is expensive.

Meet safely

Use a golf club, driving range, pro shop, or other public place when inspecting or collecting gear.

Check the condition

Inspect club faces, shafts, grips, bag zips, rangefinder screens, and any signs of repair before paying.

Avoid pressure

Be careful if a seller rushes you, refuses questions, avoids verification, or asks for unusual payment arrangements.

Keep records

Keep WhatsApp chats, payment proof, collection details, and courier tracking information until the deal is complete.

Report concerns

Contact Fairway Finds if a profile, listing, or seller behaviour looks suspicious.

Frequently asked questions

Marketplace safety questions.

How can I trade safely?

Verify the seller and inspect the golf gear before paying. Never pay for goods you have not verified.

Does Fairway Finds hold buyer payments?

No. Fairway Finds connects buyers and sellers; marketplace payments and deliveries are arranged directly.
