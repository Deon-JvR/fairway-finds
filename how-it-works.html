How it works

Simple local golf deals, approved before they go live.

Fairway Finds connects South African golfers directly. We approve profiles and listings, then buyers and sellers arrange payment, collection, or delivery with each other.

Buying

Find gear, verify the seller, then deal directly.

  1. 01

    Create your profile

    Sign up, confirm your email, and add your contact details. Admin approval keeps the marketplace cleaner.

  2. 02

    Browse approved listings

    Search by category, condition, location, and price. Featured and sponsored listings may appear higher on the page.

  3. 03

    Contact the seller

    Use the listing contact details or WhatsApp button to ask questions, request extra photos, and arrange an inspection.

  4. 04

    Complete the deal safely

    Payments and deliveries are arranged directly between buyer and seller. Verify the item before paying.

Selling

List for free, then choose paid visibility only if you want more reach.

1

Add listing details

Upload real photos, price, location, condition, category, and a clear description of the gear.

2

Wait for admin approval

Fairway Finds reviews new listings before they appear publicly on Browse and listing pages.

3

Respond to buyers

Buyers contact you directly. Agree on inspection, payment, collection, or delivery between yourselves.

4

Promote if needed

After approval, sellers can pay for featured or sponsored placement for 30 days.

Frequently asked questions

Buying and selling on Fairway Finds.

How do I buy golf gear on Fairway Finds?

Browse approved listings and contact the seller directly by WhatsApp, phone, or email.

How do I sell golf gear?

Create an approved profile, submit a free listing, and wait for admin review before it appears publicly.

Does Fairway Finds handle delivery?

No. Buyers and sellers arrange payment, collection, and delivery directly with each other.

Need help?

We can help with profile approval, listing photos, or paid placement.
