Popular TaylorMade club types

TaylorMade drivers, fairway woods, rescue clubs, irons, and wedges are often searched by golfers looking for distance and modern performance. Used listings can be a practical way to upgrade your bag.

Buying checklist

  • Confirm the shaft flex, loft, head model, and handedness.
  • Check face wear, crown marks, sole scratches, ferrules, and grip condition.
  • Ask whether headcovers, adjustment tools, or matching set components are included.
  • Meet in a safe public place or use tracked delivery only after you are comfortable with the seller.

Local marketplace safety

Fairway Finds approves users and listings, but direct transactions remain between buyer and seller. Verify the clubs, seller, and payment arrangement before completing a deal.
