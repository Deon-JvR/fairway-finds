Why buy a used golf set?

A complete or partial used set can be the easiest way to get onto the course. It is also useful when upgrading from borrowed clubs, replacing older irons, or buying for a beginner or junior golfer.

What should be included?

  • Check exactly which clubs are included, such as driver, woods, hybrids, irons, wedges, and putter.
  • Confirm shaft flex, club length, handedness, and whether all clubs match.
  • Inspect bag condition, stand legs, zips, straps, rain hood, and club wear.
  • Compare the price against condition and what is missing from the set.

Buying locally

Fairway Finds helps South African golfers connect directly. Arrange inspection, payment, and collection or delivery with the seller, and verify everything before paying.
